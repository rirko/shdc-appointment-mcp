# Pre-visit Preparation and Doctor Questions

Use this after triage or record extraction when the user may see a doctor, book an appointment, or prepare for follow-up.

## Pre-visit Checklist

Choose items that fit the record and likely department:

- Bring original reports or clear photos/PDFs, not only a summary.
- Bring prior imaging discs/files when the issue depends on imaging comparison.
- Bring medication list with dose, frequency, start/stop date, and allergy history.
- Bring recent lab results and note which items were abnormal.
- Write a short symptom timeline: onset, worsening/improving, triggers, treatments tried.
- Note current symptoms and red flags: fever, pain score, breathing, chest tightness, bleeding, jaundice, weakness, weight change.
- Confirm whether fasting is needed for planned blood tests or imaging if the hospital requires it.
- Confirm whether the target hospital requires offline first visit/建档 before online booking.
- If using the SHDC MCP flow, keep ID, mobile phone, verification access, and bound patient information ready, but do not share full identifiers in chat unless the official booking step asks for them.

## Doctor Question List

Generate 3-8 questions. Prefer practical questions that change diagnosis, treatment, follow-up, or appointment choice.

### For unclear diagnosis

- 目前最需要排除的疾病是什么？
- 现有检查能否支持诊断，还缺哪些关键检查？
- 哪些症状变化需要立即急诊？
- 应该挂哪个专科或专病门诊继续评估？

### For abnormal lab values

- 这些异常值可能与哪些疾病、药物或近期状态有关？
- 是否需要复查？多久复查？复查哪些项目？
- 是否需要调整现有药物或治疗计划？
- 哪些数值变化需要当天就医？

### For imaging findings

- 影像印象最可能代表什么，还需要与哪些检查结合？
- 是否需要增强检查、复查影像、病理或专科会诊？
- 需要多久复查一次，如何比较病灶大小或变化？
- 是否需要带原始影像文件而不仅是报告？

### For medication/follow-up

- 当前药物是否需要继续、停用或调整？
- 可能的不良反应和监测项目是什么？
- 漏服、症状加重或出现新症状时该怎么办？
- 下次复诊前需要完成哪些检查？

### For surgery/procedure evaluation

- 是否已达到手术/介入/内镜治疗指征？
- 治疗收益、风险和替代方案是什么？
- 术前还需要哪些检查或会诊？
- 术后随访和复查节奏是什么？

## Output Template

```markdown
就诊前准备
- 

建议问医生的问题
1. 
```

Do not overwhelm the user. If the list would be long, group the top items first and offer to continue.
